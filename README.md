# EdTech Growth Funnel & Revenue Optimization Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes an EdTech business funnel to identify conversion patterns, retention behavior, and revenue optimization opportunities.

The analysis simulates a real-world growth analytics scenario where business teams evaluate marketing performance, pricing impact, and user engagement across the funnel.

The funnel analyzed in this project:

**Lead → Trial → Enrollment → Retention**

---

## Business Objective

The objective of this project is to answer key business questions:

- Where are users dropping off in the funnel?
- Which marketing sources drive better conversions?
- Do weekend or weekday batches perform better?
- How do discounts influence enrollments?
- What revenue improvement opportunities exist?

---

## Funnel Analysis

The complete growth funnel was analyzed to identify drop-off stages.

**Lead → Trial → Enrollment → Retention**

### Key Findings

- Lead to Trial Conversion: **83.67%**
- Lead to Enrollment Conversion: **68%**
- Enrollment to Retention Rate: **45%**

The largest drop-off occurs between **Trial and Enrollment**.

---

## Marketing Source Performance

Performance of acquisition channels was analyzed.

Sources included:

- Facebook
- Google Ads
- Instagram
- Referral

This analysis helps identify which channels bring higher quality leads and better conversion rates.

---

## Batch Performance Analysis

Enrollment and retention performance were compared across batch types.

### Key Insight

Weekend batches demonstrate **higher retention** compared to weekday batches.

This indicates better engagement in weekend cohorts.

---

## Discount Impact Analysis

Discount impact was analyzed across income segments.

### Income Segments

- Low
- Mid
- High

### Observation

Lower income segments require **higher discounts** to convert leads into enrollments.

This provides insights into pricing strategy optimization.

---

## Revenue Analysis

Revenue was calculated based on enrollments after applying discounts.

**Total Revenue Generated:**  
₹2,811,500

This helps measure the financial impact of marketing and pricing strategies.

---

## Revenue Optimization Simulation

A scenario simulation model was created to estimate potential revenue improvement.

Using a conversion improvement slider, the model estimates:

- Optimized Enrollments
- Optimized Revenue
- Revenue Uplift

### Result

Improving trial-to-enrollment conversion can generate an estimated **revenue uplift of approximately ₹97K** without increasing marketing spend.

---

## Key Insights

- Weekend batches show stronger retention performance.
- Referral and Instagram sources show strong enrollment conversion.
- Discount strategies significantly influence enrollment behavior across income segments.
- Improving conversion rates can increase revenue without increasing lead acquisition costs.
